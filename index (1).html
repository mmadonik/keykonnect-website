The problem

Leasing calls are your biggest blind spot.

Dozens of prospect conversations happen every week at every property you oversee. You have no idea what's being said, what's being lost, or where your legal exposure is — until it's already a problem.

🏢
Asset managers: flying blind
You're accountable for occupancy performance but entirely dependent on what your property management company chooses to tell you. There's no independent view of what's actually happening on the phones. By the time you see the numbers, the damage is done.
📋
Managers: coaching without evidence
You know some agents close and some don't. But you can't prove which behaviors are costing you tours, you can't coach what you can't see, and listening to recordings one by one isn't a scalable solution — so nothing changes.
⚖️
Compliance exposure you don't know exists
One sentence about family status, occupancy standards, or protected class characteristics on a leasing call can become a human rights complaint. It happens on calls no one is monitoring. You find out when a file lands on your desk.

"We pay a property management company to lease our units. We get a monthly report with occupancy numbers. We have no idea what's actually happening on their phones — whether agents are qualifying properly, handling objections, or saying things that create legal risk."

KeyKonnect gives asset managers an independent weekly view of leasing call performance — delivered directly, without going through the 3PM.

For Directors of Leasing

"I manage eight properties and twelve agents. I know who my top performers are by feel. But I can't tell you specifically what they're doing differently, and I can't give a struggling agent concrete feedback because I haven't heard their calls."

KeyKonnect gives directors per-agent scorecards with exact call examples, coaching notes, and leasing fundamentals audits — every week, automatically.

How it works

Set it up once. Get answers every week.

KeyKonnect connects to your leasing phone line and handles everything automatically — no new hardware, no workflow changes for your agents.

1
Connect your number
Each property gets a dedicated KeyKonnect number. Calls forward to your agent's existing phone — nothing changes for them.
2
Every call is recorded
Calls are recorded with a legal notice, transcribed with speaker labels, and analyzed by AI within 2 minutes of hanging up.
3
Alerts fire instantly
If a keyword or compliance risk is detected, an alert email goes out immediately — no waiting for the weekly report.
4
PULSE report delivered
Every Monday morning, a full leasing performance report lands in your inbox. Agent scorecards, trends, coaching notes, and forward recommendations.
